About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a sophomore enrolled in the University of Michigan School of Literature, Science and Arts. Currently, I am pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Anthropology, with a minor in Asian Languages. Outside of academics, I have a broad range of interests. I am involved in an on-campus A Cappella group, Midnight Blue. Our concert theme for this semester is middle-school dance and I am so excited to perform all of these throwback songs! I also am very passionate about promoting the Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion initiative, and am the secretary of MOSAIC, the Stockwell Hall Multicultural Council. I have recently become very interested in user-friendly design and have been working on creating several aesthetic portfolio pages for myself and my friends. I also have a passion for 3D design, animation, and research. I am a research assistant for the Department of Classical Studies, and am working on developing a three-dimensional virtual representation of an archaeological site outside of Rome, Italy. In my free time, I enjoy watching anime (some of my favorites are Naruto, Hunter x Hunter, and Your Lie in April), playing Pokemon, reading (Check out my reading list here), exploring world cuisine, planning future adventures, making people laugh, and spending time with my family and friends.
